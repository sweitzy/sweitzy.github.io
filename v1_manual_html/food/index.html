We Love Food!

My sisters told me long ago that if I wanted a good woman, I should learn to cook well. I took their advice to heart! Luckily for the last 10 years, being married to Wendy, my cooking has been taken a new level and has really flourished. My best buddy Bubba has been a big influence too, and I am very grateful to him for that.

I started a 3 inch 3 ring binder over 10 years ago to keep all my recipes. Before that I had a mess of cookbooks, magazines (both with post it notes on them), and printouts. Now all the printouts are in this binder. With most recipes being online, this is very helpful.
