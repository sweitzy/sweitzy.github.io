Pioneer Woman Bacon-Wrapped Meatloaf: Everything is Better With Bacon

My best bubby Bubba made a bacon-wrapped meatloaf for our crew at a wine-tasting weekend, and it was delicious. He is really good at whipping things up without a recipe, me not so much (although I AM slowly getting better). I am an engineer and want my recipes so I don't have to memorize stuff.

It was about the same time that the awseome Pioneer Woman first appeared online. She really changed the cooking world, in my opinion, in a good way. You could get the recipes online without having to buy a cookbook. Her liberal use of pictures helped you gain confidence that you were following the recipe correctly and could replicate her results.

This recipe was one of the first I made from her first cookbook. A few thing annoy me about online recipes, specifically Food Network. You google for a recipe from a cookbook so that you can share it with a friend, and the online one varies from the one in the cookbook. I'm too paranoid to want them changing the recipe.

Notes:

  • double batch takes 1:45 to cook.
  • We have made this with gluten-free bread successfully.
