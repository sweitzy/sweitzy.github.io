What's for Dinner? Beef!

I grew up a cattle/corn farm, so we ate a lot of beef. I know the cattle industry is demonized a lot in the liberal media that I consume, and while certainly cattle (and all) farmers can and should help to reduce climate change, keep in mind we get a lot from cattle: beef, milk, cheese, leather, etc. That's a lot of good stuff!
